### RFP Notes

1. **Cloud Provider Usage**
   - To understand the overall footprint, at what proportions are each cloud provider being used?

2. **Cloud Transition Strategy**
   - Are you actively pursuing transitioning all your applications/databases to a preferred cloud provider, a multi-cloud environment, or a hybrid-cloud setup?

3. **Cloud Governance and Ownership**
   - Under which Business Unit (BU) do cloud governance, economics, and infrastructure provisioning fall?

4. **Cloud Solution Approach**
   - Do you adopt a cloud-native solution approach as a primary option, or do you prefer to utilize existing tools and emphasize solution reusability?

5. **Cloud Consolidation**
   - Are there any ongoing mergers or acquisitions that necessitate cloud consolidation?
